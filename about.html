About DuoAura Delights

DuoAura Delights is a cozy bakery dedicated to bringing you the finest homemade treats with a personal touch. We pride ourselves on using fresh, high-quality ingredients and traditional recipes handed down through generations. Our passion is to create sweet moments that brighten your day, one bite at a time.

Our Story

Started in 2018, DuoAura Delights began as a small kitchen experiment by two best friends who loved baking together. Their passion soon grew into a beloved neighborhood bakery where every pastry and cake tells a story of friendship, love, and dedication. From humble beginnings to becoming a community favorite, DuoAura is all about warmth and sweetness.

Meet the Founders

Sneha Hande

Sneha is the creative heart of DuoAura Delights, specializing in crafting innovative flavors that surprise and delight our customers. Her attention to detail and love for experimenting keeps our menu fresh and exciting.

Kinjal Gawali

Kinjal brings the timeless classics to life, perfecting recipes that have become the foundation of our bakery. Her dedication ensures every product tastes just like home, delivering comfort and nostalgia in every bite.

Why Choose Us?

We believe in the magic of homemade goodness. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or craving a simple treat, DuoAura Delights offers a variety of baked goods made with love and care. Our cozy ambiance and friendly service make every visit a delightful experience.
